SaaS Business Strategy Playbook

Strategy 1: Improve Net Revenue Retention
- Build expansion motions for healthy accounts.
- Launch account health scoring and proactive outreach.
- Tie CSM playbooks to usage and value milestones.

Strategy 2: Reduce Early-Life Churn
- Strengthen onboarding milestones in first 30-90 days.
- Trigger at-risk alerts for low activation patterns.
- Offer targeted enablement for low-adoption segments.

Strategy 3: Increase Forecast Reliability
- Use scenario planning (downside/base/upside).
- Review forecast error monthly by segment.
- Track assumptions in a shared operating log.

Strategy 4: Align GTM and Product
- Prioritize roadmap items linked to retention drivers.
- Share churn reasons across sales, success, and product.
- Build closed-loop feedback from support and NPS.

Strategy 5: Optimize Unit Economics
- Segment CAC and payback by channel and persona.
- Shift budget toward high-LTV cohorts.
- Rebalance discounts that weaken long-term expansion.

Prioritization framework:
- High impact + low effort: execute immediately.
- High impact + high effort: plan as strategic initiatives.
- Low impact + low effort: bundle into operational cadence.
- Low impact + high effort: defer unless strategic dependency.
